About Our Company

KTR Builders – Karaikal's Trusted Construction Experts

KTR Builders is a trusted construction and real estate company based in Karaikal, delivering world-class building solutions for over 28 years. We specialize in residential buildings, commercial buildings, and industrial construction projects, providing reliable and high-quality building solutions.

Our team focuses on modern architectural design, strong structural engineering, and durable construction practices to ensure long-lasting buildings. We combine proven construction techniques with modern design standards to deliver buildings that stand the test of time.

With more than 60 successful construction projects and 100+ building plans, KTR Builders has earned the trust of many satisfied clients across Karaikal and surrounding areas of Puducherry.
